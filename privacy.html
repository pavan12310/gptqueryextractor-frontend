Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 20, 2025

🔒 Short version: We don't collect your data. Your prompts and results stay on your device. We only receive payment info if you subscribe to Pro.

1. Information We Collect

GPT Query Extractor is designed with privacy as a core principle. The extension operates entirely locally in your browser and does not transmit your prompts, queries, or results to our servers.

If you create an account or subscribe to the Pro plan, we collect:

  • Email address (for account authentication)
  • Payment information (processed securely via Stripe — we never see raw card numbers)
  • Plan type and subscription status

2. How the Extension Works

All extension data is stored locally using Chrome's built-in storage API:

  • Your prompts are stored only in your browser
  • Extracted queries are stored only in your browser
  • Session history is stored only in your browser
  • Settings and preferences are stored only in your browser

Clearing your browser data or uninstalling the extension permanently deletes all locally stored data.

3. Third-Party Services

The extension interacts with chatgpt.com on your behalf — this is the core functionality. Your use of ChatGPT is governed by OpenAI's Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

For Pro subscriptions, we use:

  • Stripe — payment processing
  • Google — optional Google Sign-In authentication

4. Cookies & Tracking

Our website (this page) uses minimal, privacy-respecting analytics to understand traffic patterns (page views, referral sources). We do not use advertising cookies, cross-site tracking, or sell data to third parties.

5. Data Retention

Account data (email, subscription status) is retained while your account is active. You can request deletion at any time by emailing hello@gptqueryextractor.com. We will delete your account data within 30 days.

6. Children's Privacy

This extension is not intended for use by individuals under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Contact

For privacy-related questions, email us at privacy@gptqueryextractor.com or use our contact form.
