How to Use ChatGPT Query Extractor (Step-by-Step Guide)

March 9, 2026 · 6 min read

The ChatGPT Query Extractor is a browser extension that automates prompt execution in ChatGPT and extracts the resulting search queries. Instead of manually entering prompts one by one, it schedules multiple prompts, automatically sends them, and collects the generated queries for analysis.

What Is ChatGPT Query Extractor?

The extension allows users to send prompts automatically on a schedule, capture generated queries, export the results for research or analysis, and manage prompt workflows efficiently. It's particularly useful for SEO professionals, AI researchers, prompt engineers, and digital marketers.

Step 1 — Open and Enable the Extension

STEP 01

Open the ChatGPT Query Extractor extension in your browser. Once installed, enable it so it can begin processing prompts. The interface will appear where you can start entering prompts.

Step 2 — Enter Prompts and Optional Files

STEP 02

Enter the prompts you want to send to ChatGPT — you can add multiple at once. The extension also allows you to upload TXT or CSV files that provide extra context alongside your prompts.

Step 3 — Configure Timing

STEP 03

Set the time interval between prompts — for example, every 10 seconds, 30 seconds, or 1 minute. This scheduling ensures prompts are processed smoothly without overwhelming the ChatGPT interface.

Step 4 — Run Prompts and Extract Queries

STEP 04

Once configured, the extension begins sending prompts automatically at your chosen interval. When ChatGPT generates responses, it extracts the queries and stores them in the Results section — no manual effort required.

Step 5 — Export Extracted Queries

STEP 05

Export in multiple formats: copy to clipboard, copy formatted for Google Sheets, download as TXT, CSV, or JSON. These options make it easy to use extracted queries in spreadsheets, analytics tools, or research workflows.

Step 6 — Review History and Access Settings

STEP 06

The History section lets you review past prompts, generated queries, and previous sessions. From here you can also access the Settings panel to customize the extension.

Step 7 — Adjust Notifications, Theme, and Layout

STEP 07

In Settings you can enable or disable notifications, switch between light and dark mode, and use compact layout for a denser interface.

Step 8 — Manage Chats and Autosave

STEP 08

The extension automatically opens a new ChatGPT conversation after every 20 prompts to keep sessions organized. Autosave stores your history and extracted queries automatically — or disable it for manual control.

Step 9 — Monitor Activity

STEP 09

While running, the interface displays how many prompts are active, how many queries have been extracted, and the progress of the prompt queue. Notifications can be toggled on or off.

Step 10 — Pause, Stop, or Clear Sessions

STEP 10

At any time you can pause prompts temporarily, stop the process completely, or clear the queue and results to start a fresh session.

Best Use Cases

  • SEO Research — Extract AI-generated queries to discover keyword opportunities
  • Prompt Engineering — Test how different prompts produce different queries
  • Content Idea Generation — Turn extracted queries into blog topics
  • AI Behavior Analysis — Study how ChatGPT processes prompts and retrieves information

Final Thoughts

The ChatGPT Query Extractor makes it easy to automate prompt workflows and collect valuable query data at scale. Whether you're in SEO, AI research, or digital marketing, it provides an efficient way to analyze how ChatGPT generates and processes queries.
